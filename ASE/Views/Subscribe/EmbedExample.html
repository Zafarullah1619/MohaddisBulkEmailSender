Subscribe Form Embedding Example

Example 1: Basic Embed

Replace 36 with your Product ID:

<iframe 
    src="https://localhost:52679/Subscribe/form/36" 
    width="100%" 
    height="400" 
    frameborder="0"
    scrolling="no">
</iframe>

Usage Instructions

  1. Replace 36 in the URL with your actual Product ID
  2. Replace https://yourdomain.com with your actual domain
  3. Copy the iframe code and paste it into your website
  4. The form will automatically handle submissions and save to your database
